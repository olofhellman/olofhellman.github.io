Privacy policy



Apple's App Store distribution policy says we have to have a web page with our privacy policy. So here it is.



We are committed to not really caring about user data. Currently, we don't collect any, and we don't plan to. We really hope you'll get in touch with us by email if you find the app useful, or if you have a question, or a concern. If you do send us email, we'll know who you are! But that's the extent of the data we keep.



Sinterapt doesn't collect any user data by itself. If you send feedback about the app, we'll read it, but you can't send feedback from within the app. You'll have to send us an old-fashioned email.



If you are one of our TestFlight beta testers, Apple collects some data about your usage and sends it along to us. This includes some data about if your app crashes -- we get to know a little bit about what kind of machine you are using and some of the details of the crash. The use of this data is covered in the TestFlight user agreement.
