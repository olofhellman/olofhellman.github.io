Your privacy is important to Tomographic LLC. This privacy statement provides information about the personal information that Tomographic LLC collects, and the ways in which Tomographic LLC uses that personal information.

Tomographic LLC does not collect any personal information via its applications, including SinterAPT. If you do send us email, the email you send will be covered by this privacy policy.

Data Collection via Apple

If you have opted in to Apple's "Analytics & Improvements" data collection for "Share with app developers" (Please do! -- find this in the Setting app under "Privacy and Security"), or if you are one of our TestFlight beta testers, Apple collects some data about your usage and sends it along to us. This includes some data about if your app crashes -- we get to know a little bit about what kind of machine you are using and some of the details of the crash. The information we get from Apple is covered by this privacy policy.

If you are a TestFlight user, the use of this data is covered in the TestFlight user agreement. Otherwise, see the policy linked from the "About Device Analytics & Privacy" button in the "Analytics & Improvements" panel of the Settings app.

Thank you for considering sharing your data in this way. It really helps us improve the product.
